Seal is a private messaging app built on the Nostr protocol. Your messages are end-to-end encrypted and decentralized.

Features:
• End-to-end encrypted direct messages using NIP-17
• Decentralized - no central server can read your messages
• Works with your existing Nostr identity
• Push notifications via UnifiedPush
• Cross-platform: Android, iOS, Web, and Desktop

Seal uses the Nostr protocol for decentralized, censorship-resistant communication. Your private keys never leave your device.
